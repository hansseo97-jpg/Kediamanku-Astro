How this service works

We move from storage mapping to technical production and clean installation.

01

Storage audit

We discuss clothing volume, accessories, bags, display needs, and daily routines.

02

Measurement

We check ceiling height, wall width, corners, door clearance, and room circulation.

03

Design proposal

We prepare wardrobe layout, internal modules, material direction, and quotation.

04

Production

Panels, drawers, hardware, and lighting details are produced with careful control.

05

Installation

Wardrobe modules are installed, adjusted, cleaned, and checked before handover.
